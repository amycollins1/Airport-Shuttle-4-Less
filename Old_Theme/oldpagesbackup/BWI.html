Curbside Service

Opting for curbside service upon arriving at BWI is the most time efficient way of pickup. Domestic baggage claim at BWI is conveniently located near the exit of the airport terminal with ease of access to your ground transportation service. Available luggage porters throughout the terminal can assist with excess or large items, along with ADA transporters to assist anyone requiring wheelchair and mobility service.

Meet & Greet Service

Meet & Greet service is an exceptional option to add on to your Baltimore Washington International executive experience. Meet your chauffeur awaiting with an iPad name sign near your baggage carrousel for respective arriving flight, to assist with luggage and guide you to your vehicle. If you have reserved a Sedan or SUV, your chauffeur will guide you through BWI airport to the parking garage via the elevator and bridge walkway to exit the terminal. Please be aware, the walk from baggage claim to the vehicle can take 5-7 minutes, depending on airline travelled and baggage claim location within the airport. Not to worry, your chauffeur assists you with all baggage and personal items during the refreshing walk as you regain mobility from sitting during the flight. If you have reserved a Bus, Limousine, or Sprinter your greeter will guide you to the vehicle outside the terminal. With state-of-the-art flight monitoring, your chauffeur will be on location accordingly in case you’re arriving early or fashionably late.
