What to Expect at DCA

DC Airport – DCA, is strategically located 3 miles south of our Nation’s Capital, nestled in Arlington Virginia to serve travelers residing and visiting the Washington Metro area. Spread across 5 terminal in alphabetical order from A to E, the airport hosts 8 airlines for strategic connections to major cities and hubs. With vicinity to Washington DC, The Pentagon, and numerous federal government buildings and institutions you can expect security to be at it’s height to ensure safe and secure travel facilities. Travel in and out of DC without hassle when you book Limo Service to DCA.

Domestic and North American Flights Only

With only 8 airlines offering service at DCA, Reagan National Airport, you can find direct routes to major cities and hubs as well as remote locations as Martha’s Vineyard or Key West. International presence is held by Air Canada only which offers direct flights to Toronto and Montreal. Other operating airlines consist of Alaska, American, Delta, Frontier, JetBlue, Southwest and United.

Curbside Service

Being a smaller airport with fewer airlines, curbside service has never been more straight forward. If you are traveling Air Canada, Frontier, or Southwest, Terminal A will be the terminal you fly from or into. Terminal A is the original building of DCA and only has 1 floor, so you enter and exit from the same doors to depart or arrive. In case you are flying a different airline from Terminals B – D, you will depart from the upper level to check in and arrive at the lower level with baggage claim.

No Meet & Greet Service, Only Greeter Service

DC Airport, DCA Reagan National Airport features an urban designed layout for security purposes of Washington DC and surrounding government buildings. With parking being very minimal, Meet & Greet service is not an available option at DCA. Travelers would have to walk through the terminal and major traffic just to reach the parking lot. Therefore, curbside service at DCA Reagan National Airport is the best option for travelers on a budget. If you enjoy the exclusivity of Meet & Greet, we do offer Greeter Service for a higher cost than Meet & Greet where an individual greeter awaits on you at baggage claim and guides you through the airport to the limo curb where your chauffeur will whisk you away to your itinerary.
