Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) sets forth the practices, procedures, and obligations of Airport Shuttles 4 Less (“Company,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) regarding the collection, use, storage, disclosure, and protection of information provided by individuals (“Customer,” “you,” or “your”) who utilize or access our website www.airportshuttles4less.com, book our services, or otherwise engage with us.
By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Purpose and Scope

1.1. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to affirm and clarify that Airport Shuttles 4 Less does not collect, store, or misuse any personal identifying information, including but not limited to credit card data, phone numbers, and email addresses beyond what is strictly necessary to provide requested transportation services.

1.2. This Policy applies to all interactions, whether digital or verbal, that occur between you and Airport Shuttles 4 Less, including but not limited to:

  • - Website visits.
  • - Online reservations.
  • - Customer service inquiries.
  • - Payment transactions processed via third-party payment processors.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

2.1. Airport Shuttles 4 Less explicitly affirms that it does not:

  • - Retain or archive credit card information.
  • - Collect government identification numbers (e.g., Social Security, driver’s license, passport).
  • - Gather sensitive health-related or biometric data.
  • - Create customer profiles or behavioral tracking databases.

2.2. Any information voluntarily provided (such as name, phone number, or email) is strictly limited to the scope of service delivery and is not used for any unrelated commercial purpose.

3. Financial Transactions and PCI Compliance

3.1. All financial transactions are conducted exclusively through PayPal, a certified third-party payment processor.

3.2. PayPal is a PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) compliant provider. By relying solely on PayPal:

  • - Airport Shuttles 4 Less never views, stores, transmits, or retains credit card information.
  • - All sensitive financial information is processed securely through PayPal’s encrypted systems.

3.3. Customers can be assured that our Company is fully compliant with PCI requirements through PayPal’s authorization and certification.

4. Voluntary Customer Information

4.1. Customers may voluntarily provide certain limited details in the course of:

  • - Submitting an online booking inquiry.
  • - Contacting us by phone or email.
  • - Confirming pick-up and drop-off locations.

4.2. Such information may include:

  • - Name, email address, and phone number.
  • - Service preferences (e.g., time, date, vehicle type).
  • - Pick-up and drop-off addresses.

4.3. This information is utilized solely for the purpose of:

  • - Confirming and completing transportation services.
  • - Providing driver instructions.
  • - Ensuring smooth communication regarding bookings.

5. Storage and Retention of Information

5.1. We do not maintain permanent databases of personal information.

5.2. Limited contact information, when shared, is used only for the immediate duration of the booking and customer support.

5.3. Once the service is fulfilled, customer information is either deleted or securely anonymized, except where retention is required by law (e.g., for tax or accounting purposes).

6. Disclosure of Information

6.1. Airport Shuttles 4 Less does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose customer information to any third party for marketing or unrelated commercial use.

6.2. Information may only be disclosed in the following limited circumstances:

  • - To assigned chauffeurs or subcontracted drivers strictly for operational purposes.
  • - To comply with applicable legal obligations, court orders, or governmental regulations.
  • - To protect the rights, property, or safety of Airport Shuttles 4 Less, its employees, or its customers.

7. Data Security Measures

7.1. Airport Shuttles 4 Less implements industry-standard safeguards, including but not limited to:

  • - Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption for all website transactions.
  • - Restricted access to operational booking systems.
  • - Internal policies governing confidentiality and limited employee access to customer information.

7.2. Notwithstanding the above, no system is entirely immune to risk. Customers acknowledge that transmission of information via the internet carries inherent risks, though we endeavor to minimize such risks to the fullest extent possible.

8. Cookies and Website Analytics

8.1. Our website may employ minimal cookies for functionality (such as language preferences or booking session continuity).

8.2. We do not use cookies for:

  • - Behavioral advertising.
  • - Retargeting.
  • - Profiling customers for marketing purposes.

9. Children’s Privacy

9.1. Airport Shuttles 4 Less does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

9.2. Our services are intended for adult customers booking transportation services.

10. Customer Rights

10.1. You have the right to request access to any personal data you have voluntarily provided.

10.2. You have the right to request deletion of such information at any time by contacting us.

10.3. You have the right to refuse to provide personal details, with the understanding that doing so may limit our ability to provide certain services.

11.1. Airport Shuttles 4 Less reserves the right to modify, amend, or update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
